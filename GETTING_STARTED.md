## The Core Problem THE RECORD Solves

Modern governance fails because:
- **Institutional Lag** - Data is weeks/months old and filtered through political agendas
- **Hidden Incentives** - Corruption, capture, and gatekeeping
- **Information Asymmetry** - Those with power hide information
- **Fear-Driven Dynamics** - People can't speak truth without retaliation
- **No Accountability** - Officials make predictions but face no consequences when wrong

## How THE RECORD Works Differently

### 1. **Influence (λ) Cannot Be Bought**
Power in THE RECORD is earned through **predictive accuracy**, not money or popularity.
- If you report signals that get verified → your influence (λ) grows
- If you report noise or lies → your influence is slashed
- This creates evolutionary pressure where observant, honest people rise naturally

### 2. **Anonymous Input + Public Aggregation**
You don't vote "yes/no" on whether to fix a road. Instead:
- You **witness** and submit: "Road has 4-inch deep fissure at these coordinates"
- System aggregates signals anonymously
- Patterns emerge without social pressure, groupthink, or retaliation

### 3. **4-Layer Anti-Group-Failure Architecture**

```
L1 (SIGNALS)     → Private anonymous observations
                   Why: Prevents groupthink & retaliation
                   
L2 (PROPOSALS)   → Distributed constraint validation
                   Why: No single expert/AI/authority can dominate
                   
L3 (TRACKING)    → Prediction accountability mesh
                   Why: Rewards accuracy, not charisma
                   
L4 (META)        → Independent oversight layer
                   Why: Detects system drift & capture attempts
```

### 4. **H3 Geospatial Blurring (Epistemic Privacy)**
When you submit a signal:
- ❌ System does NOT record your exact GPS coordinates
- ✅ System identifies the H3 hexagonal cell (~500m radius)
- **Result:** Proves issue exists in the area WITHOUT revealing which house you live in
- **Protection:** Prevents "retaliatory governance" and protects whistleblowers

### 5. **Black Box Flight Recorder**
Every action is:
- Time-stamped
- Cryptographically hashed
- Linked to previous hash (blockchain-like)
- Stored on IPFS (decentralized, uncensorable)
- Publicly auditable

**Why this matters:** Institutions can't suppress failures or claim "nobody could have known" when THE RECORD predicted it.

## How to Use THE RECORD

### First Time: Create Your Account

1. **Click "Create Account"** button in the header
2. **Generate Cryptographic Keys**
   - Creates Ed25519 key pair (like a mathematical passport)
   - Zero-knowledge privacy: anonymous but verifiable
   - Non-transferable influence: can't be bought or sold

3. **Build Humanity Score** (verify you're human, not a bot)
   - Email verification: +15 points
   - Phone verification: +20 points  
   - Location verification: +10 points
   - GitHub verification: +25 points
   - **Need 30+ points to submit signals**
   - **Need 60+ points to bond influence**
   - **Need 80+ points to validate proposals**

4. **Optional: Professional Verification**
   - For engineers, inspectors, officials, etc.
   - Provides enhanced credibility
   - Still operates within system rules (no override power)

### Submitting a Signal (Reporting an Issue)

1. **Navigate to Your Area**
   - Click on Bubble Map to drill down: Global → Continent → Nation → State → City → District
   - OR use Satellite Map view and navigate to your location

2. **Click "Submit Signal"** (requires 30+ humanity score)

3. **Fill in the signal:**
   - **Category:** Infrastructure, Water, Safety, Energy, Healthcare, etc.
   - **Description:** Be specific and factual (avoid opinions)
   - **Location:** 
     - Click "Add Location (H3 Blurred)" to use current position
     - OR click directly on mesh cells in the Satellite Map
   - **Anonymity:** Keep anonymous (default) to prevent social pressure

4. **Location Verification** (Anti-VPN)
   - System runs multi-signal verification
   - Checks: Timezone match, VPN detection, Tor detection, IP consistency
   - **Why:** Prevents manipulation from fake/remote submissions
   - **Privacy:** Your exact location is still blurred to H3 cell

5. **Submit**
   - Signal goes to L1 (raw signals)
   - System looks for clustering with other signals
   - When enough signals cluster → becomes a Problem (L2)

1. **Select a Problem** from the L2 view
2. **Click "Submit Proposal"**
3. **Required Fields:**
   - Title

   - **Predictions** (MANDATORY):
     - Expected outcome

4. **Distributed Validation:**

5. **Prediction Accountability:**
   - Your predictions are recorded in the Black Box
   - When proposal completes, reality is compared to predictions

   - **Skin in the game:** Poor predictions = lost influence

Click "IPFS Storage" button to:
- **Initialize:** Start IPFS node in your browser
- **Auto-backup:** Automatically store signals/problems/proposals to peer-to-peer network
- **Pinning:** Keep data alive on the network

- **Why:** Cannot be censored or deleted by any single entity

## Why These Design Choices?

### Why Anonymous Signals?
**Problem:** Public submission → groupthink, bullying, retaliation  
**Solution:** Private input → honest data without social distortion  
**Example:** Teachers can report school issues without fear of administrative blowback

### Why Distributed Validators (not single experts)?
**Problem:** Single authority → capture, bias, gatekeeping  
**Solution:** Multiple independent validators must agree  
**Example:** Proposal for "24/7 public transit" triggers budget/legal/safety/energy validators simultaneously

### Why Prediction Requirements?
**Problem:** People propose solutions without accountability  
**Solution:** Must predict outcomes → tracked → influence adjusted  
**Example:** Politician promises "crime will drop 50%" → we measure it → reality determines their future weight

### Why H3 Geospatial Blurring?
**Problem:** Exact GPS → identity exposure → retaliation risk  
**Solution:** Hexagonal cell (~500m) → proves signal from area → protects individual  
**Example:** "Water smells bad in H3 cell 8928308280fffff" proves area problem without revealing which house

### Why Black Box Event Log?
**Problem:** Institutions hide failures and rewrite history  
**Solution:** Immutable hash-chained record → can't be altered or suppressed  
**Example:** When institutions fail, public can prove THE RECORD warned them months earlier

### Why VPN/Tor Detection?
**Problem:** Attackers can submit fake signals from anywhere  
**Solution:** Verify location signals match claimed area  
**Example:** Someone in Russia can't submit 1000 signals about New York potholes

### Why Professional Verification?
**Problem:** Not all observations are equal (engineer vs random person on bridge safety)  
**Solution:** Verified professionals get weighted influence while system maintains safeguards  
**Example:** Licensed structural engineer's bridge report carries more weight, but can't override consensus

**No.** Professionals get:
- ✅ Weighted influence (their signals count more)

- ❌ **NO override power**
- ❌ **NO ability to block consensus**

### "How do I know my data is safe?"
**Transparency is the safety:**
- All data is public (by design)
- Hashes prove integrity
- IPFS prevents deletion
- Cryptographic signatures prove authenticity
- **You** control your private keys (sovereign identity)
